Next steps — phase 2

Tax return ratio is the most rigorous long-run performance metric but requires realized outcome data to measure honestly. It belongs in a retrospective evaluation layer — comparing projected EDC inputs against actual economic outcomes after a deal has run its cycle. Over time, tracking prospective EDC scores against realized tax revenue and job creation builds a calibration discount factor by deal type, turning this model into a living institutional tool rather than a static scorecard.
